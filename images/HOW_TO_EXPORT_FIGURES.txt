Put your MATLAB plot images in this folder (PNG works best).

Export a figure from MATLAB with:

    exportgraphics(gcf, 'lab1_fig1.png', 'Resolution', 200)

or in a Live Script: right click on the plot -> Save As -> PNG.

Then insert it in the .tex file (the images/ prefix is not needed,
LaTeX already searches this folder):

    \labfigure{lab1_fig1.png}{fig:l1f1}

and refer to it in your text as:

    ... a small leakage can be seen (\figref{fig:l1f1}).

Naming suggestion: lab1_fig1.png, lab1_fig2.png, lab2_fig1.png, ...
